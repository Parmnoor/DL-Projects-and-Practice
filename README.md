## About

I believe that building genuine depth in deep learning theory and implementation is the necessary next step in my progression toward AI engineering. This repository is where I intend to do that work, openly and incrementally.

## Roadmap

The following outlines the topics I intend to work through, roughly in order. I do not expect this to be strictly linear, as certain projects will naturally pull me forward into adjacent concepts.

**Phase 1 — Foundations**
- Neural network fundamentals and backpropagation from scratch
- Loss functions, gradient descent variants, and learning rate scheduling
- Regularization techniques: dropout, batch normalization, weight decay

**Phase 2 — Core Architectures**
- Convolutional neural networks and image classification
- Recurrent networks, LSTMs, and sequence-to-sequence models
- Attention mechanisms and the transformer architecture

**Phase 3 — Frameworks and Tooling**
- PyTorch: autograd, custom modules, training loops, and deployment basics
- TensorFlow and JAX: comparative practice and ecosystem familiarity
- Experiment tracking with tools such as Weights & Biases

**Phase 4 — Applied Projects and Research**
- End-to-end personal projects with real datasets
- Reproductions of foundational papers (e.g., ResNet, Attention Is All You Need, DDPM)
- Exploration of domain-specific applications relevant to my professional work
